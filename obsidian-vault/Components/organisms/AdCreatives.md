# Ad Creatives / Sponsorship Ads

Retail media ad preview component. Renders a visual mockup of a sponsored product tile, banner, or display ad unit as it would appear on the retailer's storefront.

## Variants

| Variant | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Product Tile | Sponsored product card with badge |
| Banner | Wide horizontal display unit |
| In-grid | Native product grid placement |

## Usage
- Used in ad creative review, campaign setup previews, and creative management screens
- Always show actual product image + title + sponsor badge
- Not for interactive ad serving — display/review only
